# Design System: Minimalist Luxury (B&W)

## 1. Visual Identity
- **Palette**: Strictly Monochrome.
  - **Background**: Pure White (`#FFFFFF`) or Ultra-Light Slate (`#F8FAFA`).
  - **Primary Text**: Deep Black (`#111827`).
  - **Secondary Text**: Mid-Slate (`#64748B`).
  - **Accents**: Light Slate (`#F1F5F9`) for borders and backgrounds.
- **Corners**: Consistent **32px** border radius for all cards, sections, and primary images.
- **Buttons**: Fully rounded **Pill** shapes (`rounded-full`). High contrast (Black background / White text).

## 2. Typography
- **Headings**: **Outfit** (Bold/Medium).
  - **Scale**: Professional & Clean.
  - **Hero Heading**: `text-5xl` to `text-7xl` max.
  - **Section Heading**: `text-4xl` to `text-5xl`.
  - **Card Heading**: `text-xl` to `text-2xl`.
- **Body**: **Inter** (Regular/Medium).
  - **Scale**: `text-base` to `text-lg`.
  - **Tracking**: Standard (no extreme letter-spacing).

## 3. UI Components
- **Filter Tabs**: Pill-shaped buttons with solid state changes.
- **Cards**: Minimalist with 32px radius and subtle borders (`border-slate-100`).
- **Overlays**: Clean black/white gradients or solid blurs. No extreme editorial overlapping text.
- **Interactions**: Smooth spring-based transitions (Framer Motion).

## 4. Brand Tone
- Professional, Sophisticated, High-End Boutique.
- Focus on **Clarity** and **Artisanal Quality**.
- Avoid "Magazine/Editorial" clutter or extreme typographic sizes.

## 5. Administrative Style
- The CMS preview and internal dashboard should mirror the site's B&W Minimalist Luxury aesthetic for brand consistency during content creation.
